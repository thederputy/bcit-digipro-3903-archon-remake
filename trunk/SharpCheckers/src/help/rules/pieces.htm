The Standard Men

  • The Standard men, technically described as Black & White, must be light and dark (say red and white, or black & white), turned, and round, not less than one & one-eight inches, nor more than one and one-quarter inches in diameter.

Placing the Men

  • The men shall be placed on the Black squares.
  • The Black men shall invariably be placed upon the first twelve squares of the board; the White upon the last twelve squares.
